FAQ what's uncommon hacks? Uncommon Hacks is a weekend-long celebration of technology where college students from everywhere come to Chicago to make fun, random, and innovative tech-related projects with friends old and new. You’ll work in a small team to design and build a creative project from start to finish in a weekend, learning and having fun in the process. what's a hackathon? Hackathons are hacking marathons where attendees are encouraged to collaborate on a project. Hackers will be in a fun, supportive environment with the necessary tools provided to create excellent projects. Newbies encouraged, normies aren’t. i’ve never been to a hackathon before. can i come? Yes, yes, YES. APPLY!!! do i need a team? Nope, you can hack solo! but we encourage you to meet new friends and create teams. There will be a designated time to meet other hackers. how much does it cost? NOTHING. attendees do not need to pay anything. uncommon hacks will provide wifi, meals, swag, workspace, and limited transportation reimbursement (TBD). when is uncommon hacks? february 10 - 11, 2018 at the Polsky Exchange, 1452 E 53rd St, 2nd Floor, Chicago, IL, 60615. what can i build? build the most creative projects you can think of. this is a safe space to create the weirdest, the quirkiest, and the most amazing projects ever. what should i bring? attendees should bring a valid form of ID. other recommended items are: laptops, blankets or sleeping bags, toiletries, chargers, and whatever might bring you more success! what if i have another question? email celery@uncommonhacks.com what's special about uncommon hacks? thats for us to know and for you to figure out. trust us to create an event that will be h y p e. don’t believe us, check out our 2016 video! how big can teams be? teams can be as big as 4 and as small as 1 i'm an international student. can i attend? absolutely, the event will accept international students! uncommon hacks will do its best to reimburse students that require travel reimbursements. i'm a high school student. can i attend? you have to be over 18 to attend. we encourage you to apply regardless. code of conduct? we abide by the major league hacking code of conduct.
